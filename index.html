How To Coffee

This is a step by step guide on how Jake makes coffee using the french press.

Step 1:Fill The Kettle

  • Fill to the Max Fill line inside the Kettle.
  • Use the best water avalible.
  • Press both the On/Off and the Hold Button.
  • This should take about 5 Minutes.

Step 2:Weigh and Grind Beans

  • The Ratio is "1 gram coffee to 17 grams water".
  • As of 2/13/2022: Jake has been using ~42 grams of coffee.
  • Weigh on the scale in bowl. Make sure to zero out the scale with the empty bowl on it before you weigh the beans.
  • Turn on the grinder first and then dump in the bean. This improves the life of the burrs...or something.

Step 3:Start Brewing

  • Add gound coffee to the empty french press.
  • The water should boiling or about 210 degrees farenthiet. The brew temp you want is between 205 and 195.
  • At 1 to 17: 42 grams of coffee should have 731 grams of water.
